About Creative Branding Solutions

A Lagos-born branding and print house helping ambitious businesses look as good as they perform.

Our Story

When You Say Jump, We Say How High

Creative Branding Solutions started with a simple frustration: too many great Nigerian businesses were being let down by slow printers, inconsistent colours and vendors who disappeared after the invoice was paid.

Today we're a full-service branding and production partner — designing, printing and installing everything from a single business card to a nationwide billboard and fleet-wrap campaign. We understand the essence of corporate branding and the need for quality products. We go the extra mile rather than say a thousand sorry.
